Hi! My name is Lara, and I am a PhD student in Linguistics at the University of Edinburgh, based at the School of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences.

I grew up in Bilbao, the Basque Country, a bilingual community. I was raised bilingual in a One Parent One Language environment: my mother only spoke to me in Spanish and my father only spoke to me in English.

I learnt Basque as an L2 at school and French in high school. During my BA, I completed an Erasmus+ programme in Bologna, Italy, where I learnt Italian. I am currently learning Turkish.
