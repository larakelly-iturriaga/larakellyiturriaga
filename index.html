Hi! My name is Lara, and I am a PhD student in Linguistics at the University of Edinburgh, based at the School of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences.

I grew up in Bilbao, the **Basque Country**, a bilingual community. I was raised bilingual in a One Parent One Language environment: my mother only spoke to me in _Spanish_ and my father only spoke to me in _English_. I learnt _Basque_ as an L2 at school and _French_ in high school. During my BA, I completed an Erasmus+ programme in Bologna, Italy, where I learnt _Italian_. I am currently learning _Turkish_.
